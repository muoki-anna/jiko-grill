About us...
Jiko-Grill

At Jiko‑Grill,we fuse bold,open‑flame grilling techniques with East African–inspired spices and fresh, local ingredients for an unforgettable dining experience.

Why dine with us?
  • Smoky, soulful flavors: From perfectly marinated nyama choma‑style meats to flame‑grilled fish and seasonal veggies, each dish bursts with depth and character.
  • Farm‑fresh, locally sourced: We partner with Kenyan farmers for produce that’s sustainable, bold, and remarkable in taste.
  • Vibrant yet laid‑back atmosphere: Gather around communal tables, relax on our outdoor patio, enjoy fire-lit warmth and a friendly buzz—ideal for dates, casual dinners, or group celebrations.
  • Inclusive menu: Hearty vegetarian grilled dishes and gluten‑free options ensure every guest can enjoy big flavor.
  • Thoughtful beverage pairings: Sip craft Kenyan beers, signature cocktails, and natural juices. The crowd favorite: our house “Grill Smash” cocktail—bright, herb‑infused, and refreshing.
  • Local flair, cosmopolitan polish: We’re inspired by Kenyan grilling culture and present it with modern flair—flavorful, stylish, unpretentious.
  • Welcoming service: Attentive, knowledgeable, warm—ensuring your meal is relaxed and memorable.

Perfect for: meat lovers craving rich, fire-cooked food; groups who enjoy lively communal meals; anyone seeking flavorful grilled dishes in a welcoming, stylish setting.
